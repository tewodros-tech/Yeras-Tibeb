The cloth
remembers.

Yeras Tibeb means the beauty of heritage. We work with weavers and makers in Ethiopia to keep the language of handwoven cloth alive, then give it room to move through the world.

Every thread carries a story — of family, of land, of quiet resilience. From the highlands of Wollo to the streets of Addis, our pieces are a bridge between past and future.
