About Us

The story behind the cloth.

Yeras Tibeb is more than a brand — it's a movement to preserve Ethiopian heritage through the art of handwoven textiles.

Founder & Creative Director

Liya Demissew

/ˈliːjə dɛˈmɪsuː/

Liya Demissew is the visionary founder of YERAS TIBEB, a brand born from her deep love for Ethiopian heritage and a passion for preserving the ancient art of handwoven textiles.

Growing up in Addis Ababa, Liya was surrounded by the rich tapestry of Ethiopian culture — the vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and the stories woven into every piece of fabric. Her grandmother, a master weaver, taught her that each thread carries a story of family, land, and resilience.

After studying fashion design and sustainable business practices abroad, Liya returned to Ethiopia with a mission: to create a brand that honors tradition while embracing modernity. YERAS TIBEB — meaning "the beauty of heritage" — is the realization of that dream.

Today, Liya works directly with master weavers across Ethiopia, ensuring fair wages, safe working conditions, and the preservation of centuries-old techniques. Her vision is to share Ethiopian craftsmanship with the world, one piece at a time.

Every piece of cloth tells a story. My grandmother taught me that when you weave, you're not just making fabric — you're preserving memories, honoring ancestors, and creating something that will outlive us all. — Liya Demissew

Our Values

What drives us

These principles guide everything we do at YERAS TIBEB.

Preserving Heritage

We are committed to keeping Ethiopian weaving traditions alive for future generations through education and fair partnerships.

Sustainability

We use organic, natural materials and traditional dyeing methods that honor the earth and minimize environmental impact.

Empowerment

Our Journey

The story so far

From a dream to a movement — here's how YERAS TIBEB came to life.

1

2018 · The Vision

Liya Demissew returns to Ethiopia after studying fashion design abroad, inspired to create a brand that celebrates Ethiopian heritage through handwoven textiles.

2

2019 · Building Community

Liya begins working with master weavers across Ethiopia, building relationships and learning the ancient techniques passed down through generations.

3

2020 · The First Collection

YERAS TIBEB launches its first collection — a small batch of handwoven garments that blend traditional Ethiopian design with modern silhouettes.

4

2022 · Growing the Family

The brand expands to work with 12 master weavers, creating sustainable livelihoods and preserving weaving traditions in communities across Ethiopia.

5

2024 · Global Recognition

YERAS TIBEB gains international recognition for its commitment to ethical fashion, sustainable practices, and preserving Ethiopian cultural heritage.
